核心内容

1. Dynamic Programming 动态规划

(A tool / an algorithm to solve a class of problems)

The basic problem (P1):

① An underlying discrete-time dynamic system. e.g., $x_{k+1} = A x_k + B u_k + w_k$ .

$$
x _ {k + 1} = f _ {k} (x _ {k}, u _ {k}, w _ {k}), k = 0, 1, \dots , N - 1.
$$

② A cost function is additive over time

$$
J = E \left\{g _ {N} (x _ {N}) + \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {N - 1} g _ {k} (x _ {k}, u _ {k}, w _ {k}) \right\}.
$$

$$
J = E \left\{\prod_ {k = 0} ^ {N} g _ {k} (\cdot) \right\}
$$

Goal: minimize J by choosing $u_{0}, \cdots, u_{N-1}$ .

Solution: use dynamic programming!

2. Example. You are the owner of a 7-11 store. Consider a very simple scenario. You only sell one product (e.g., cocoa).

$x_{k}$ : no. of cans available at the beginning of the k-th day.

Uk: no. of cans ordered

Wk: no. of cans sold on the k-th day

${x}_{k + 1} = {x}_{k} + {u}_{k} - {w}_{k}$ .

$r(x_k): penalty. (e.g., r(x_k) = |x_k|^2)$

Cuk : purchasing cost. βwk : profit.

$R(x_{N})$ : terminal cost.

② $J = E \left\{ R(x_{N}) + \sum_{k=0}^{N-1} \left( r(x_{k}) + c u_{k} - \beta w_{k} \right) \right\}.$

Goal: choose $U_0, \cdots U_{N-1}$ to minimize $J$
