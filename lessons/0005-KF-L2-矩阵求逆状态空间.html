1. Matrix Inversion Lemma


For any matrices $A, B, C, D$ with compatible dimensions,

b A, c nonsingular, then
A + b (D) 可逆.

$$
(A + B C D) ^ {- 1} = A ^ {- 1} - A ^ {- 1} B (C ^ {- 1} + D A ^ {- 1} B) ^ {- 1} D A ^ {- 1}
$$

Proof: step ① $(I+p)^{-1}=(I+p)^{-1}(I+p-p)=I-(I+p)^{-1}\cdot p$ [e1]

$$
P + P Q P = P (I + Q P) = (I + P Q) P \quad | e _ {2} |
$$

$$
p (I + Q p) ^ {- 1} = (I + P Q) ^ {- 1} \cdot p \tag {e3}
$$

Step 2 $(A+BCD)^{-1}=(A(I+A^{-1}BCD))^{-1}$

$$
= (I + A ^ {- 1} B C D) ^ {- 1} \cdot A ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
= (I - (I + \underbrace {A ^ {- 1} B C D}) ^ {- 1} \cdot \underbrace {A ^ {- 1} B C D} _ {P}) \cdot A ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
= A ^ {- 1} - (I + A ^ {- 1} B C D) ^ {- 1} \cdot A ^ {- 1} B C D A ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
= A ^ {- 1} - (I + A ^ {- 1} B C D \cdot A ^ {- 1} \cdot A) ^ {- 1} A ^ {- 1} B C D A ^ {- 1}

e _ {3} = A ^ {- 1} - A ^ {- 1} \underbrace {A C D A ^ {- 1}} _ {P} \cdot (I + B C D A ^ {- 1}) ^ {- 1}
